Leesburg, Virginia

Location:

A nice warm area in Northern Virginia; Very close to Washington DC. 3 - 4 hours from State College.

About:

Weather:

Warm, sometimes hot and humid. Not a lot of snow, unfortunately.

Roads:

Very wide, clean, new, and relaxing. Many are well known, and the scenic routes with single lanes are pleasant to look around in, drive in, and race in.

Wildlife

LOTS of deer and squirrels. Blue jays, rabbits, cardinals, and bears also present sometimes. Foxes and skunks also prevalent in the area. Did I mention more deer?

Things to do:

  • Sight seeing
  • Driving around on the nice roads
  • (professionally) Recklessly driving on the nice roads
  • Visit the many amazing campuses in Virginia
  • Go to a massive MGM area that includes a casino.
